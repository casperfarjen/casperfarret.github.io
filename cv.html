About Me

My interests and skills range from the field of astrophysics to instrumentation. Throughout my research, I design and build high-tech instruments for (ultra-)stable, high-resolution spectroscopy; silicon-based meta-materials; and study astrophysical processes underlying (exo-)planetary evolution. I enjoy the work on novel solutions for high-tech instrumentation to shed light on the laws that govern our Universe.
