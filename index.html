About Me

Exoplanetary researcher at the Observatory of Geneva, focusing on instrumentation. Currently, I am in charge of the NIGHT project: an instrument and survey for the measurement of exoplanet helium atmospheres to study the origins of the observed exoplanet population. I have a strong interest in astronomical instrumentation and exoplanet astrophysics. Besides astrophysics, I care about the social aspects of our work - free access to knowledge and equity and inclusion.

My research interests include:

  • Astronomical instrumentation design
  • High-resolution spectroscopy
  • Exoplanetary atmospheres
  • Exoplanet formation and evolution
